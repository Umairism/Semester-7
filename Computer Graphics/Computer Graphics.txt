    Visibility              |   No          |   There isnt an organized set of elements, This site rush bunch of elements to user.

    Minimalist Design       |   No          |   The page is heavily cluttered with ads, links and content competing for attention.
    Help Recover Errors     |   No          |   No clear error messages or guided recovery when something goes wrong (e.g. broken links).
